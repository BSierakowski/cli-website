keypad.txt

=============

CONGRATULATIONS for successfully finding the easter egg on our website!

Here's how to make use of the Root Ventures branded keypad:

(1) Flip the keypad over. Using the provided knife, slice through one of the traces on the back of the board that corresponds with the OS that your computer is running:

  • MacOS = trace 1
  • Windows = trace 2
  • Ubuntu = trace 3

** tip for non-electrical engineers: press down very hard with the knife, and scrape multiple times until you are sure the horizontal line of copper is fully broken.

(2) Plug into your computer.

(3) Enjoy!

(4) Download the Arduino IDE and play around with mapping the keys to make them do whatever you want! Original code can be found here: https://github.com/rootvc/macro_keyboard
